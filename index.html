Okay, so I guess I do need some text here. I will use this opportunity to talk about one of my favorite pass times, indie games! My love for indie games began long ago when I download a lil’ online game marketplace called Steam.

What is Steam?

Steam is a great application for purchasing, playing, and managing videogames online! Let’s face it; the days of ethical DMR free software are long gone (sorry g2g). For better or worse consumers today need almost certainly need a client for online gaming. Though this model has its issues, one plus is indie developers now have a platform that can be profitable.
